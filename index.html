Research Interest

 I work in the field of hyperspectral image processing, multi-source remote sensing classification and fractional signal processing. Currently, I focus on the following research topics:
  • Multi-source remote sensing classification
  • Fractional signal processing with its application in hyperspectral image processing
  • Hyperspectral image anomaly detection
